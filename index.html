Goals

Jesse’s lifelong goal is to fight ignorance, including his own. He is excited by maths and people in general. Explaining difficult mathematical concepts is satisfying to him. His current goal as a teacher is to be a successful maths teacher. In the next few years, Jesse aspires to improve his skills in higher level mathematics and computer science. He is guided by his family, beliefs, and pursuits. He has learned many valuable lessons over the years, but most importantly that “the line between good and evil cuts through the heart of every human being.” In his search for knowledge, he remembers that good is what sets us apart and makes us special.
